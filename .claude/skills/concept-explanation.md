# Concept Explanation Skill

## Purpose
Provide clear, structured explanations of robotics and AI concepts for the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook.

## Capabilities
- Break down complex concepts into digestible parts
- Provide concrete examples for abstract ideas
- Use analogies to clarify difficult concepts
- Include visual description prompts where appropriate

## Usage
- Apply to any complex technical concept
- Maintain technical accuracy while improving clarity
- Ensure explanations are accessible to target audience
- Support different learning styles
